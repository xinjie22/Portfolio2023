STILL LIFE

TITLE

Still life

YEAR

2022

MEDIUM

Pencil, Charcoal stick & Charcoal pencil

DESCRIPTION

In this still life arrangement, wooden mannequins represent the daily routine of humans in this society, doing the same thing day in and day out which working like a robot repeating the instructions, and working without any emotions. Fruits symbolize colour and happiness, adding color and pleasure to this boring life, which will become the motivation and happiness in our life. I choose this image because of the composition, particularly the space between each subject and the contrast lighting to highlight the subject.
