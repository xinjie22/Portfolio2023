TITLE

Echeveria

YEAR

2022

MEDIUM

Pencil & Artline

DESCRIPTION

In view of its tough nature, I think the plant represents persistence and strength, making it a nice gesture for someone going through a difficult time. The neutral grey-green tones represent plainness and simplicity, which means that a simple and unpretentious life can be also very happy. It tells me life doesn't have to be colorful to be considered life.
